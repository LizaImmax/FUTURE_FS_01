Mood Journal

Description

Empower your emotional well-being with daily mood tracking and thoughtful journaling. The Mood Tracking and Journaling Web App offers users a comprehensive platform for managing their emotional well-being. Users can create accounts, log in, and then track their daily moods, adding contextual notes if desired. The app includes a journaling feature for recording thoughts and experiences, complete with formatting and tagging options.

Features

  • Daily mood tracking with contextual notes
  • Account creation and secure login
  • Journaling with formatting and tagging options

Learning Objectives

  • Designing a user-friendly platform for emotional well-being
  • Implementing secure authentication and user management
  • Developing features for mood tracking and journaling
